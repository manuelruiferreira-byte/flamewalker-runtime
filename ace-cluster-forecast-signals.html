State Meanings
Forward states:
FUTURE_GATE means a coin becomes resonant within the horizon. WATCH_EARLY means the gate exists and movement is starting. PRE_IGNITION means future gate plus market ignition. IGNITION means active resonance plus market confirmation. CHASE_RISK means the move is already extended.
Divergence states:
FLUSH_WATCH means future resonance plus a strong dump. CAPITULATION_CANDIDATE means the dump has volume or intensity. RECLAIM_IGNITION means the flush was absorbed. BROKEN_SIGNAL means weakness was not rejected.
